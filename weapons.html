Then there’s the question of where the new weapons slot in. So, behold, our latest tier list for Season 4(our opinion!).

Recommended Loadout

We recommend V9S, LH1 or XP-54 for light.
For meduim it's AKM, FCAR or Model 1887
And for heavy it's Lewis Gun LMG, SA1216 shotgun or .50 Akimbo

Too pair with that we recommend the Evasive Dash, Stun Gun, Breach Charge, and Vanishing Bomb for Light.
Medium should try the Healing Beam, APS Turret, Defibrillators, and the Jump Pad.
The Winch Claw, RPG-7, Barricade, and Dome Shield are great options for Heavy contestants.
